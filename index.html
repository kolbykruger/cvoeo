Our Mission

CVOEO provides vital services to over eleven thousand households (over 25,000 individuals) every year. Our Housing Advocacy services (Fair Housing Project, Mobile Home, and Vermont Tenants) serve residents statewide.

CVOEO employs a dedicated staff of approximately 200 employees and engages more than 1,100 volunteers annually, working out of approximately 18 locations in addition to Head Start classrooms throughout the Champlain Valley region.
